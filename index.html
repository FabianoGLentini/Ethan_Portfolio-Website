About Me

Freelance sound designer with a passion for the interactive quality of audio in video games. As a kid, my cousin shared with me his love for electronic music which opened my mind to the conceptual process of “programming” music. Once I had discovered video games as a teen and began to understand the potential of the interactivity offered by them, I was hooked

Since then I have completed a Bachelor of music at the University of Toronto and a Sound Design and Production Post-graduate degree at George Brown College. Through those programs and extra-curricular projects I have developed experience with a wide variety of industry tools from game engines (Unreal Engine 4&5, Unity, Source).

In my spare time I also enjoy experimenting with technical sound design prototypes and composing music.
